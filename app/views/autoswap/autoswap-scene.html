About This Fetaure
The 'Auto Swap Ringtone' feature changes your ringtone to a random one from your downloaded list at the frequency you choose.

FEATURE LIMITATIONS:

1.) Swap ringtone after each call cannot be done.
2.) The lowest interval possible is 5 minutes.
3.) If you download ringtones with an older version of this app, please download them again. Only works with tones it has tracked.
4.) You cannot auto swap to ringtones installed outside of this application.
